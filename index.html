How does it work?

Create a color palette in 4 steps:

1. Select your colors for text and background.

2. Choose your buttons. Primary is mostly for header navigation selections. Secondary is for less important buttons and info cards.

3. Accent color is an additional color. It is good for highlights, hyperlinks, boxes, cards, etc.

4. Finished? Press on “Export” to download a .zip file with your color palette in .png and your color codes in a .txt file.
